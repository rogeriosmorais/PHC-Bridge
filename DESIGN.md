The resources: if you have simple renderization you end up with idle GPU resources that could be used in animation and human body simulation, but all modern games do it on the CPU, which is not optimal.

The scope: two human bodies fighting

The tech available: an Intel i7-14700 and a 4070 SUPER

The goal: make full use of the available resources to make the best animations possible in real-time.
